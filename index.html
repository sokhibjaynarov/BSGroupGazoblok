BS Gazobloklari Galandiyaning AIRCRETE texnologiyasi bilan!

BSGroup kompaniyasi O'zbekiston hududida avtoklavlangan gazoblokini ishlab chiqarish bo'yicha yetakchi hisoblanadi. Zavodimiz 2023-yilda tashkil etilgan, zavod yiliga 450,000 kubometr mahsulot ishlab chiqarish quvvatiga ega. BSGroup Galandiyaning AIRCRETE ishlab chiqarish texnologiyalari bilan jihozlangan zamonaviy zavod bo'lib, avtoklavda mustahkamlashgan gazobloklarini ishlab chiqaradi.

Bizning xizmatlar

Biz sizga yuqori Jahon standartlariga javob beruvchi mustahkam, ekologik toza mahsulotlarni ishlab chiqaramiz va yetkazib beramiz.

Yetkazib berish

Biz sizga materiallarni turli transport vositalari, temir yo’l, yuk mashinalari, turli yuk ko’tarish quvvatiga ega kran-plitalar orqali etkazib bera olamiz.

Onlayn buyurtma berish

Onlayn buyrtma berish xizmati mavjud, siz veb-saytimiz orqali buyurtma berishingiz mumkin, kerakli tovarlar hajmini va kontaktlaringizni ko’rsatasiz, menejerimiz siz bilan bog’lanadi. Siz tovarlar, xizmatlar uchun masofadan turib to’lashingiz mumkin va mahsulotlarimiz sizning qurilish maydonchangizga yetkazib beriladi.

Materiallar hajmlarini hisoblash

Bizga murojaat qiling va biz sizga qurilish uchun qancha gazoblok kerakligini aniqlashga yordam beramiz.

How much will it cost?

To get a better idea of the cost of what you want to build, give us a quick phone call. We’ll ask you a few questions about the nature of the site, what sort of interactivity the site will have, your graphic design needs, etc. Then we’ll be able to give you a ballpark figure. If you’re still interested, we’ll come to your place of business and come up with a firm quote.

How long it takes to design?

The time limit of any assignment is normally dictated by the client. If you have any time limit in mind we will attempt to assemble it for you. The main general delay in the making of a website is waiting for the content of the pages to be sent to us by the client.

How do I associate with IT Company?

The process begins when you contact us with your requirements. We analyze your requirements and respond to you. On the basis of the further discussion, you can choose an engagement model that suits you the best. After that, we begin the process of development.

When do I pay?

For most projects, equal payments are made at the start, midway, and the end of the project, but we can work with you to set up a schedule that meets your needs.
